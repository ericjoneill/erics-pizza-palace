Eric's Pizza Palace

"Where else but Eric's?"

Since Eric's doors opened in 2007, Eric's Pizza Palace has prided itself in providing quality slices and exceptional service by their team of chefs who identify together as a family. One of the proudest achievements of Eric's Pizza Palace was being featured on Chef Gordon Ramsey's Kitchen Nightmares during its final season. With a wide variety of pizza toppings even for the pickiest of eaters, Eric's Pizza Palace is sure to sate anyone's hunger. Come visit your favorite pizza shop's favorite pizza shop today.
